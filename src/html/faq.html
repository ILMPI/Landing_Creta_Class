Here are three ways to purchase your courses:

  1. Click the "Try it now" button above to purchase your Trial Lessons;
  2. Purchase courses on the official website;
  3. Download the "Creta Class" app and purchase your desired courses in the app.

Refunds are subject to our official refund policy. If you wish to request a refund, please contact our support team with your purchase details. Please note that eligibility and the refund window may vary based on the product and payment platform. We strive to handle all refund requests promptly and fairly.

You can find your purchased courses in the following ways:

  • Official Website: Log into your account on the official website. Your purchased courses will be listed in your dashboard or "My Courses" section.
  • Creta Class App: Open the app, sign in with the account you used to make the purchase, and navigate to your courses or learning center.
  • Email Confirmation: Check your inbox for a purchase confirmation email, which includes instructions and links to access your courses.

Courses typically start immediately after your payment is processed.

If you bought through the website or app, you should have instant access.

For scheduled live or cohort-based courses, you'll receive details via email about the start date and how to join your first lesson.
